About Me

Hello! My name is Adam Schoenberg. I'm a current student at the University of Texas at Austin studying Computer Science in the Turing Scholars Honors program, and I'm originally from Houston. Last summer, I interned at NASA Johnson Space Center, where I worked with robotics engineers to develop a web application for a motor database. Aside from computer science, I actively participated in the FIRST Robotics Competition throughout high school, competing with Team 118, the Robonauts. I also played cello in my high school orchestra, finishing second in the TMEA state competition twice, and I've studied piano for over ten years. Other hobbies of mine include hiking, photography, and watching sports.
